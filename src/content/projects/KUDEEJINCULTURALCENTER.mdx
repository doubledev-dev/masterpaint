designDetailsEN: "Kudi Chin: Moving Forward with a Living Community through Three Pillars of Sustainable Development: Social, Economic, and Cultural. These pillars will help propel the Kudi Chin community forward sustainably.Social Aspect: Promote love and pride within the community. This includes preserving and sharing cultural heritage through exhibitions that tell the story of three religions and four beliefs, as well as through workshops on traditional sweets. A crucial element is the creation of communal spaces where community members can gather, exchange ideas, and foster unity.Economic Aspect: Encourage income generation for the elderly and other community members by selling goods locally and participating in storytelling through exhibitions.Environmental Aspect: Implement recycling of waste into bricks for building fences, pathways, and counters to reduce waste. Additionally, adopt construction methods that use affordable materials and involve community members, utilizing common materials within a unified framework to reflect diversity while maintaining harmony."
